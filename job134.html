Disney Jobs from Home - WFH

Job Type: Freelance/Part-Time

Salary: Starting at $0.25 per word

Company: Inside the Magic (Entertainment News Website)

Location: Work from anywhere

About the Role

Inside the Magic is the number #1 site online for Walt Disney World, Disneyland, Universal Studios, and more. We provide entertainment news on Disney, Pixar, Marvel, and Star Wars films, covering theme parks, movies, TV, video games, conventions, special events, and more.

We are looking for innovative, creative, and passionate freelance writers to pitch and write stories for our platform on an article-by-article basis.

How to Apply

To apply, please email your pitch and relevant writing samples to pitches@insidethemagic.net. Our rates start at 25 cents per word, and we use fixed fees. We are an inclusive platform and encourage writers from diverse backgrounds to pitch us.

At this time, we are primarily interested in personal essays and long reads related to entertainment news. However, we welcome a variety of pitches, so don't hesitate to send us your ideas, even if they don't fall under these categories.

Pitching Guidelines

  • Email your story idea to pitches@insidethemagic.net.
  • If your pitch is timely, add "Urgent" to the subject line.
  • We may take a few weeks to respond, but please be patient.
  • Make sure your pitch is concise and catchy to cut through the volume of submissions.

Additional Information

We welcome diverse voices and encourage you to share unique perspectives in the entertainment space. Inside the Magic offers a flexible, remote opportunity where your creativity can shine in the world of Disney and beyond.
