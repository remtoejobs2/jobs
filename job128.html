Job Description

Wells Fargo is seeking a Senior Consumer Credit Card Customer Service Representative. Learn more about careers and business opportunities at Wellsfargojobs.com.

Job Role:

  • Promote customer success and help search for appropriate questions for customers and services via several communication channels like numbers, text messages, and discussions.
  • Ask questions, apply rules and regulations, solve daily problems, and offer the best customer service.
  • Handle regular inquiries, address issues, and solve difficult customer problems.
  • Research customer issues, confirm details, and collaborate with other departments to resolve concerns.
  • Act as a liaison with knowledgeable personnel to address complex issues.
  • Train and assist lower-level staff.
  • Respond to user queries and manage their problems effectively.

Necessary Skills:

  • Minimum of six months of relevant experience or equivalent education and training.
  • Ability to offer high-level service, data management, and problem resolution.
  • Strong interpersonal skills and the ability to work with all levels of customers and management.
  • Basic Microsoft Office skills.
  • Experience working in a fast-paced, deadline-driven environment.
  • Effective verbal, written, and interpersonal communication skills.
  • Attention to detail and ability to handle multiple computer systems.
  • Ability to quickly learn new processes and business concepts.

Job Benefits:

  • Health benefits: Medical, dental, and vision insurance.
  • Retirement plans: 401(k) with company contributions.
  • Paid Time Off (PTO): Holidays, vacation, and personal days.
  • Employee Stock Purchase Plan (ESPP): Discounted company stock purchase.
  • Training support: Career development and reimbursement programs.
  • Flexible work arrangements: Work from home options and flexible hours.
  • Employee discounts on financial services and company products.
  • Life insurance and disability benefits.
  • Parental leave policies for new parents.
  • Employee Assistance Program (EAP) for personal and work-related support.
  • Community involvement opportunities for volunteering and service projects.
