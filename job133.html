Description

The Visual Originator contributes to realistic, marking, and format plan development. This individual also contributes to the conceptual work for products, packaging, branding, marketing, and other promotional platforms.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Collaborates with team leaders and planners to meet project goals in graphic design and development.
  • Creates strong designs across a variety of projects including branding, typography, and packaging.
  • Pitches creative ideas to team leaders and creative facilitators.
  • Meets client requirements and department standards while adhering to deadlines.
  • Stays updated on current design trends and works with a collaborative team approach.
  • Proactively solves problems and adapts to additional responsibilities.

Requirements:

  • Strong understanding of design and print production.
  • Ability to work independently with minimal supervision.
  • Proficient in Adobe Creative Suite and Microsoft Office.
  • Strong typography, branding, and design skills.
  • Excellent problem-solving and time-management skills.
  • Bachelor's degree in Graphic Design or a related field required.
