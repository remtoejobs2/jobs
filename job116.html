National Pharmaceutical Account Director - Remote

Apply Now

Deerfield, IL

Responsible for developing partnerships with global biopharmaceutical manufacturers to enhance competitiveness in the market and establish the Company as the retail and specialty pharmacy of choice.

Job Responsibilities

  • Develops and fosters relationships with global pharmaceutical manufacturers with the focus on establishing/expanding alliances in both the retail and specialty distribution markets.
  • Leads the process for gaining access to LDD (limited distribution drugs) for Specialty Pharmacy with assigned manufacturers.
  • Develops specific acquisition and adherence strategies for retail prescription products in collaboration with internal support teams and manufacturer partners.
  • Responsible for development and implementation of joint strategies focused on Rx DTC marketing, adherence services, data services, market insights, and unique distribution/purchasing strategies that enhance sales for the Company and our pharma partners.
  • Collaborates with manufacturer trade relations and brand teams to proactively identify business opportunities and implement tactics to establish enterprise-wide business relationships.
  • Negotiates and contracts with pharmaceutical manufacturers for service programs in the retail and specialty arenas.
  • Functions as the primary point of contact between the biopharma industry and internal stakeholders.
  • Utilizes internal/external business intelligence to provide the early identification of new business opportunities.

  • Bachelor’s Degree and at least 6 years of experience in account management in the pharmaceutical industry or trade relations OR a High School Diploma/GED and at least 9 years of experience in account management in the pharmaceutical industry or trade relations.

  • Experience developing strategic initiatives which align with business goals and budget.

  • Knowledge of the pharmaceutical manufacturer business segment and the cost variables involved within the segment.
