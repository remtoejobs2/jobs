Position Summary:
The Telepharmacy Technician is responsible for all daily operations of a Telepharmacy Remote Dispensing Pharmacy location. This role builds upon the skills of a Pharmacy Technician, focusing on managing daily pharmacy operations, building patient loyalty, and maintaining excellent customer service.

Key Responsibilities:
• Oversee the safe operation of the Remote Dispensing Pharmacy.
• Manage pharmacy inventory, including controlled substances.
• Communicate with Supervising Pharmacy/Pharmacist.
• Handle customer service, prescription fulfillment, and patient interaction.
• Collaborate with team members to resolve any customer concerns and improve business metrics.

Required Qualifications:
• Minimum 1+ year experience as a Pharmacy Technician.
• PTCB National Certification or equivalent state-accepted national Pharmacy Technician Certification.
• High school diploma or GED.
• State-level licensure requirements may vary by state.
• Strong customer service and communication skills.
• Ability to focus on tasks for extended periods without distractions.

Preferred Qualifications:
• Experience in Inventory Specialist Role.
